# Rick and Morty

Rick Sanchez is a brilliant but alcoholic and nihilistic scientist who invents portal gun technology. He frequently goes on adventures with his grandson Morty Smith across infinite dimensions and realities.

## Characters
- **Rick Sanchez**: The smartest man in the universe, known for his catchphrase "Wubba Lubba Dub Dub"
- **Morty Smith**: Rick's nervous and moral grandson who often gets dragged into dangerous interdimensional escapades
- **Jerry Smith**: Morty's father, often portrayed as incompetent
- **Beth Smith**: Morty's mother and Rick's daughter, a horse surgeon
- **Summer Smith**: Morty's older sister

## Themes
The show explores themes of existentialism, nihilism, family dysfunction, and the consequences of unchecked scientific advancement through dark humor and science fiction adventures.
